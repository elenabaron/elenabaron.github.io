About

Artist born in the cradle of great Spanish painters, Ayamonte (Andalucía). His art is innate, she took her first steps in Seville, where she spent the most of her life, winning his first prize for painting at 6 years of age and receiving his first lessons in art school. In her youth she studied Agricultural Engineering and then moved to Madrid, where he currently resides, to work as an engineer, until he decides to leave everything behind and resume his passion for art. Artist with a unique style, without external influences, great personality and originality, especially playing with the figurative and the abstract. Very versatile in his work, constantly learning, experimenting and searching for new techniques. She feels free and so his art is also very free, without committing to a definite style or a single way of painting. Her works come from her soul; sometimes more calmer, sometimes more euphoric; sometimes more patient, sometimes more restless; sometimes more nostalgic, sometimes more hopeful. But always rebellious and dreamy. The inspiration of his work lies mainly on their rich inner world; from his travels; nature, influenced by her contact with her since childhood, as well as studying agronomy and even in his other passion, food. Always present the harmonies of color, textures and reliefs. In many of his creations, there are difficult messages to grasp at first glance, unconsciously generated, making criticism (of an imperfect world), (of how you see your world and the world around him) something beautiful, magical and simple . His works are in private collections in Spain and abroad (United Kingdom, Germany, USA, Mexico …).
